How has joining Las Guerreras had an impact on you life?
"I faced discrimination when I tried to join a football team. I went to a club, and they immediately rejected me. They never even let me show them how well I could play. Now, Las Guerreras has become my second family. They gave me the chance to play, and the team had a great positive impact on my life."
